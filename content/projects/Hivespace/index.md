# Hive Space
Hivespace is a study platform that helps you take notes, create quizzes, and track your study time. You can share your progress with groups and set goals, allowing your peers to keep you accountable and motivated. Having a dedicated platform for sharing notes between study groups and having AI generated quizzes to test you skill and show off how much you have learned. This project was developed as part of senior design over the fall 2025 and spring 2026 semesters.

## Technologies
node.js (backend)
Expo (React Native)
Mongo DB (Online shared notes, Users data and group data)
SQL lite (local storage of notes to make them searchable)
Clerk (authentication)
